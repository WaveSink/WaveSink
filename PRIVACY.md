# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the **WaveSink** desktop application.

## 1. Zero Data Collection

**WaveSink does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, telemetry, usage statistics, or any other information in any form whatsoever.** 

The application is designed to respect your privacy fully. It functions entirely locally on your Windows machine, does not require an internet connection to operate, and does not communicate with any external servers.

## 2. Local Audio Processing

All audio routing, equalization, and volume control happen strictly on your local hardware. WaveSink interacts with the Windows audio APIs (WASAPI) to perform its functions but never records, saves, or transmits your audio streams.

## 3. Open Source Transparency (GPLv3)

WaveSink is free and open-source software distributed under the **GNU General Public License v3.0 (GPLv3)**. Because the source code is public, our commitment to your privacy is completely transparent and verifiable. Anyone is free to inspect, review, and compile the source code themselves to confirm the absence of any data collection, tracking, or analytics mechanisms.

## 4. Changes to this Policy

Because WaveSink is structurally designed not to collect data, we do not anticipate any material changes to this privacy policy. However, should the project evolve, any updates to this policy will be clearly documented in the repository.

## 5. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or the application's practices, please open an issue in the official WaveSink GitHub repository.
